# offshore-field-feed_ai_v004

Purpose: Operation center field-feed background for the Web MVP.

Mode: built-in image generation.

Prompt:

Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: wide 16:9 in-game central Operation field-feed background for an offshore wind maintenance strategy game
Primary request: Create a realistic offshore wind O&M field camera image from the wet aft deck of a service operation vessel, looking toward a working offshore wind farm in Taiwan-Strait-like sea conditions. The scene should feel practical, industrial, and usable inside a game UI center panel.
Scene/backdrop: open sea, sea state 2 to 3, gray-blue misty daylight, wet steel deck, yellow safety railings, realistic offshore work equipment, distant turbine array.
Subject: a few clearly readable conventional offshore horizontal-axis wind turbines, each complete visible rotor has exactly three smooth tapered blades attached to one centered hub and evenly spaced 120 degrees apart; tower, nacelle, hub, shaft axis and monopile/foundation must be physically plausible.
Style/medium: photorealistic industrial maritime photography, no anime, no painting, no illustration, no UI overlay.
Composition/framing: wide cinematic 16:9, horizon in upper middle, foreground deck gives scale, central readable turbine but not too close, enough contrast for UI overlay.
Lighting/mood: overcast maritime daylight, wet surfaces, light sea spray, professional O&M atmosphere.
Constraints: no people, no text, no logo, no watermark, no fantasy machinery; turbines must be structurally correct and not blocked by foreground objects.

Negative guardrails:

two-bladed wind turbine, four-bladed wind turbine, five-bladed wind turbine, extra turbine blade, missing blade, bent blade, warped blade, kinked blade, twisted blade, detached blade, floating blade, off-axis rotor, off-center hub, uneven blade spacing, malformed nacelle, duplicate nacelle, split tower, leaning tower, impossible offshore foundation, impossible ladder, impossible platform, turbine blades intersecting tower/nacelle/platform/each other, turbine growing from deck, duplicated horizon artifacts, readable letters, numbers, signage, HUD, decorative frame, character, human figure.

Manual visual QA:

- Central visible turbine has exactly three readable blades.
- Hub, nacelle, shaft axis, tower and foundation are visually plausible.
- Background turbines are small but conventional three-bladed rotors.
- No people, text, logo, watermark, UI frame or fantasy machinery.
